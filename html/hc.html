Few Words About Us

Home Clinic is a project created by 3 studants of ESMAD, Bruno Sério, Catarina Fonseca and Jialong Ye. They attend to the course named "Licenciatura em Tecnologias e Sistemas para a Informação Web" .

The main goal of this project is to provide medical appointements to everyone in the comfort of their home and with that we can avoid the tedious waiting lines in health centers.

During the process of making the web application the students used html,css and javasript in Visual Studio Code.
